explicit

Creates a KeyRepeatTimer. These timers take a function in the keyPressed which will be hit once on key-press, once after the initialDelayMstime has elapsed, and then repeatedly at intervals specified by therepeatDelayMs` time. Use this to allow users to do things like press-and-hold an arrow key to move a cursor across a keyboard.

Parameters
initialDelayMsthe initial time in milliseconds to wait before repeating the key-press action.
repeatDelayMsthe interval time to repeat the key-press action after the initial delay has elapsed.

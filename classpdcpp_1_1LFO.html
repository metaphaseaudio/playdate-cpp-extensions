void pdcpp::LFO::setBias ( float  center)

Sets the center point of the LFO. LFOs are typically treated as oscillating between +/- depth (with some exceptions for oscillators like arpeggiators,) and this adds a constant to the value, "biasing" the LFO toward the value.

Parameters
centerthe new center value of the LFO
